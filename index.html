Our Story

Richi Rich Restaurant has been a beloved part of the Aliganj community in Lucknow for years, serving up authentic North Indian cuisine that brings families and friends together. From our signature Pav Bhaji to our comforting Veg Thali, every dish is crafted with care to create those special moments that turn ordinary meals into cherished memories.

Whether it's a small birthday celebration, a casual family dinner, or just satisfying your cravings for street food favorites, we're here to make every visit memorable. Our affordable prices and warm, inviting atmosphere make us the perfect choice for budget-conscious diners who don't want to compromise on quality or taste.
